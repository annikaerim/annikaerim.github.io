About

Annika Rim is a 2024 graduate of the University of San Diego, where she earned a degree in Business Administration with a minor in Theology and Religious Studies. She has been awarded multiple academic scholarships in recognition of her academic excellence.

Born and raised in San Diego, California, Annika later moved to Seattle, Washington to pursue new opportunities. With over six years of experience in data-driven operations, Annika specializes in management analytics, financial reporting, and workflow optimization. She has a proven ability to manage multiple priorities, analyze complex datasets, and implement solutions that improve efficiency.

Annika is proficient in a range of tools and platforms including Tableau, AppFolio, Quicken, Deepnote, zipForm, and Microsoft Excel (Certified Microsoft Office Specialist). She also holds a valid California Real Estate Salesperson license.
